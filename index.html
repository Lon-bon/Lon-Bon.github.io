Purpose Statement

Unform Your Bias™ exists to provide tools to overcome bias and collectively reduce the amount of it in the world. We will have two focuses: Communities and Education.

Communities

  • Engage citizens with different viewpoints in collaboration circles within their communities to tackle challenges, together

  • Teach community circle participants to unform their biases and work together to solve the problems their communities face

Education

  • Offer curriculum at all levels to teach school age children to prevent bias formation, and seek peaceful resolution without violence or bullying

  • Teach teachers how to build skills for collaboration, influence and problem solving in themselves and their students
